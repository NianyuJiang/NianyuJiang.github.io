I myself is gradually becoming a heavy AI user starting at 2024, after ChatGPT has released. I used to learn AI by the earlist at 2018, when AI was mostly used to do image classification and speech detection, however, the giant leap both at hardware, software and methodology make the LLM type of AI tools born, significantly reshaped how I am doing the engineering work.

Without question, "using AI to find the correct answer" is not guaranteed. As a engineer R&D, the absolute correct answer itself may not exist, since the question I am exploring at has no confirm answer yet. Thinking on what AI can help me conclude the current solutions is actually becoming what I mostly relied on.
